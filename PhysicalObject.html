Class: PhysicalObject

PhysicalObject

Represents a physical-object and its intrinsic properties. This is useful for approximating extended objects as a uniform, continous body mass (ignoring its particulate composition).

PhysicalObject

Represents a physical-object and its intrinsic properties. This is useful for approximating extended objects as a uniform, continous body mass (ignoring its particulate composition).
